The ritual · 3 steps

Hang it once.
Hear it forever.

01 ~2 min · one time only

Hang it

Loop the pre-tied cord over any hook, nail, or branch. Fence post, pergola, doorframe — wherever the breeze moves, it works.

02 ~5 min · optional

Tune it

Slide the cord loops to adjust tube spacing. More gap for a softer, slower tone. Tubes closer together for a livelier ring. It's yours to dial in.

03 Every day · indefinitely

Let it sound

No batteries, no switches, no effort. The wind does everything from here. Your garden now has a voice.
